Privacy Policy

My Happy Place (MHP) values your privacy. We collect only essential information needed to provide services under the Botswana Entrepreneurial Transformation Program (BETP).

  • Data Collection: We may collect names, contact details, project submissions, and participation data.
  • Purpose: Information is used to improve engagement tools, verify participation, and provide personalized updates.
  • Data Protection: All data is securely stored using encrypted systems, compliant with Botswana’s Data Protection Act (2018).
  • Third-Party Access: We do not sell or share data. Only authorized MHP staff and partners have controlled access for BETP reporting.
  • Opt-Out: You may request data removal at any time via support@myhappyplace.co.bw.

By using My Happy Place, you agree to engage respectfully and uphold the principles of transparency, innovation, and community-driven transformation.
