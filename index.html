Welcome to My Happy Place: Together, We Build Botswana’s Future

My Happy Place (MHP) embraces the President’s call for bold action to drive Botswana’s economic transformation by aligning its strategies with the nation’s vision for transition, transformation, innovation, entrepreneurship, and digital growth.

MHP not only amplifies the transformation narrative and agenda but also provides a platform called Send`a a colloquial term meaning “send”, that enables everyone interested to actively participate, gain recognition, and be visible in an engaging way. While enhancing the reach and impact of the Botswana Economic Transformation Program (BETP), My Happy Place is also advancing digital sovereignty through platforms that engage citizens, global citizens, and the Diaspora of Botswana.

These efforts demonstrate MHP’s dedication to building independent, sustainable systems and opening immediate partnership opportunities. By leveraging Send`a, the MHP website, and collaborating with The Social Connect , MHP positions Botswana as a pioneering testbed for citizen participation in the nation’s transition toward a knowledge-based economy that benefits all.

Why My Happy Place?

Botswana stands at an economic crossroads, moving boldly beyond its diamond-dependent past toward a diversified, inclusive future. MHP empowers every citizen to be a driver of this transformation, helping to create jobs, build local industries, and shape a thriving, resilient economy.

We offer best-practice benchmarks to support projects to attain BETP flagship project status by applying scientifically based approaches and computational frameworks for objective guidance.
